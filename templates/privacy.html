Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Data Collection

DataViz Pro processes all data locally within your browser session. We do not collect, store, or transmit your uploaded datasets to any external servers. All analysis is performed in-memory and data is cleared when your session ends.

2. Session Data

Your uploaded data is stored temporarily in server memory for the duration of your session. This data is automatically cleared when you close the browser or after a period of inactivity.

3. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to maintain your analysis state. This cookie contains only a session identifier and no personal data.

4. Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact us through the application support channels.
